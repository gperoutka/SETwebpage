CBR Testing

We have increased our capacity for running CBRs to 21 specimens. The California Bearing Ratio test is usually run in conjunction with a standard proctor test. The CBR test requires a 4 day soaking period, generally we are able to provide a week for a turn around time. We run our CBR test in general accordance with ASTM:D1883.
